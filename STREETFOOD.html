Indian street foods are known worldwide for their taste, diversity and easy accessibility in various cities like Mumbai, Delhi, Kolkata and Bangalore to name a few. Few street dishes like kathi rolls, daulat ki chaat, chhole bhature and misal pav are quite famous. Food fans, traders and aspiring start-ups; you’ve come to the right place. Street food is open to anyone, anywhere, and right now the industry is exploding.
Whether you’re looking for somewhere to grab a bite to eat on your next night out, or dream of being the chef behind the cooker of your very own street trading unit, we can help.
